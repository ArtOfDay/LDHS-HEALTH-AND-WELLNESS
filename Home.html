Health and Wellness for Students

Health and wellness play a key role in the growth, learning, and happiness of teenagers.During high school years, students go through physical, emotional, and social changes.These changes affect focus, behavior, and academic performance.Good health supports steady energy levels during long school days.Mental wellness supports clear thinking and emotional control.Students with healthy habits manage school pressure more effectively.Proper sleep improves memory and attention in class.Balanced meals support brain function and physical strength.Regular physical activity reduces stress and improves mood.Strong wellness habits build confidence and independence.These habits prepare students for responsible decisions in adulthood.

1. Physical Health

•Proper Nutrition

-A balanced diet gives teenagers the energy they need for studying, sports, and daily activities. Teens should aim to eat:

-Fruits and vegetables for vitamins and minerals

-Whole grains for energy

-Protein such as fish, eggs, beans, and lean meat for growth and muscle repair

-Plenty of water to stay hydrated

-Limiting junk food, sugary drinks, and excessive fast food helps prevent fatigue and health problems.

•Regular Exercise

Physical activity keeps the heart strong, improves mood, and reduces stress. Teenagers are encouraged to get at least 60 minutes of physical activity daily, such as:

-Sports (basketball, volleyball, soccer)

-Walking, jogging, or cycling

-Dancing or fitness workouts

-Exercise also improves sleep and boosts self-confidence.

2. Mental and Emotional Wellness

•Managing Stress

-Schoolwork, exams, and social pressure can cause stress. Healthy ways to manage stress include:

-Time management and planning tasks

-Taking short breaks while studying

-Practicing relaxation techniques like deep breathing

-Talking to friends, teachers, or family members

•Positive Self-Esteem

-Teenagers should learn to appreciate their strengths and accept their imperfections. Positive self-esteem helps teens:

-Make better decisions

-Handle challenges confidently

-build healthy relationships

-Avoid comparing yourself to others, especially on social media, and focus on personal growth.
